About LTS

LTS was founded in 2022, and it was build on our absolute love for tacos! We hope that our little shop adds a spark of joy and happiness to this little town! In LTS, we are one big loving family that has trust in eachother and believe that food and good tacos are what brings people together. We hope to put a smile on your faces one taco at a time! LTS was formed with a vision in mind. That vision was the create a better future and a brighter community. Everyday, the LTS family works harder to make that vision come true, so stay calm and enjoy a platter of our famous tacos!
